    The Readercon Restaurant Guide (PDF, 406kB)

    What is this?

    This is the mobile guide to Readercon 24. It's a web page that works like a mobile app, so your browser should cache all of it and make it work when you're offline as well. The guide should work on most computers, phones and tablets that have some sort of relatively modern web browser, though on Windows phones the "Next" view may be buggy.

    What's at work here is the magic of the HTML5 cache manifest. In order to make said magic work, about 1MB of your browser's memory is being used (probably without telling you anything about it). To clear that cache, you'll need to look at your browser settings, then something like Advanced > Website settings > [this site] > Clear stored data (details depend on your browser, OS, etc.)

    My name is Eemeli Aro, and you can reach me at eemeli@gmail.com. This guide is an instance of KonOpas, an open-source project I started for providing conventions with easy-to-use mobile guides. I also do a few other fannish things as well, like chair a bid for a Worldcon: Helsinki in 2015!
